# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Kindergarten children in the class, guided by parents at home on mobile phones. Parents need a low-friction way to open the day's English material, let the child listen and repeat, and record completion.

## Product Purpose

This product turns daily teacher-supplied English materials into large, child-friendly point-and-repeat lesson cards. Success means a child can finish today's lesson independently with minimal distractions, receive encouragement, and build a visible check-in streak. Older published lessons should remain available for review.

## Brand Personality

Warm, clear, encouraging. The interface should feel like a calm classroom activity for young children, not a marketing page or a dense adult learning system.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, tiny controls, decorative clutter, dark gamer-style interfaces, and flashy animations that distract from listening and repeating.

## Design Principles

- One learning item per screen, with large readable text and generous touch targets.
- Parents should immediately understand whether they are opening today's lesson or reviewing an older one.
- Children get simple, positive feedback after completion.
- Guest mode may preview learning, but personal progress and recordings stay limited to enrolled students.
- Admin and publishing workflows stay practical and plain, optimized for getting daily content online quickly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Prioritize mobile readability, high contrast, large tap targets, reduced visual noise, and layouts that work in WeChat, iOS Safari, and Android Chrome. Motion should be minimal and never required to understand the lesson.
